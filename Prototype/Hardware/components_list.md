# Component List

---

### `Core Processing Components`

- **Raspberry Pi**  
  Used as the main processing unit for handling image input, decision logic, and system coordination.

- **Microcontroller Board**  
  Responsible for low-level control tasks such as motor operation, relay switching, and sensor data collection.

- **Camera Module**  
  Mounted on the robot to capture clear images of plant leaves during movement.

---

### `Motion and Drive Components`

- **4-Wheel Chassis**  
  Provides mechanical support and mobility for navigating row-based farm paths.

- **DC Motors (4 units)**  
  Used to drive the wheels and control forward, backward, and turning movements.

- **Motor Driver Modules**  
  Interface between the microcontroller and motors to control speed and direction.

---

### `Spraying System Components`

- **Pesticide Storage Tank**  
  Stores liquid pesticide for controlled spraying.

- **Pumping Motors (2 units)**  
  Responsible for pushing pesticide from the tank to the sprinklers.

- **Sprinklers / Nozzles**  
  Used to deliver pesticide in a focused and controlled manner.

- **Relay Module**  
  Acts as an electronic switch to turn the pumping motors on and off.

---

### `Sensing Components`

- **Soil Moisture Sensor**  
  Measures moisture levels in the soil.

- **Soil Condition Sensors**  
  Used to monitor basic soil parameters that affect crop health.

---

### `Power Management Components`

- **Rechargeable Battery (12V)**  
  Primary power source for the entire system.

- **Buck Converter / Voltage Regulator**  
  Converts battery voltage to required levels for electronic components.

- **Power Distribution Wiring**  
  Ensures safe and stable power delivery to all modules.

---

### `Communication Components`

- **Wireless Communication Module**  
  Enables data transfer between the system and the monitoring interface.

- **Connecting Cables and Interfaces**  
  Used for communication between processing unit, microcontroller, and sensors.

---

### `Mechanical and Supporting Parts`

- **Mounting Brackets**  
  Secure components such as the camera, tank, and sensors.

- **Fasteners and Frame Accessories**  
  Provide structural stability.

- **Protective Enclosures**  
  Protect sensitive electronics from dust, moisture, and field conditions.

---

### `Notes`

All components are selected with affordability, availability, and ease of replacement in mind. The system is designed to be assembled using commonly available parts to ensure practicality for real-world agricultural use .
